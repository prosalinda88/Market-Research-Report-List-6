<p><p>The gypsum-fiber board market comprises two primary types: wood fiber and glass fiber. Wood fiber gypsum boards incorporate cellulose fibers from wood, offering enhanced thermal insulation and soundproofing properties, making them ideal for residential and commercial applications. On the other hand, glass fiber gypsum boards utilize fiberglass reinforcement, providing superior strength, fire resistance, and moisture control, suitable for high-performance environments such as hospitals and laboratories. Both types cater to diverse construction and building material needs, emphasizing sustainability and durability.</p></p>
