<p><p>The Polyamide 12 market can be categorized based on its production methods: polycondensation and ring-opening polymerization. The polycondensation method involves the stepwise reaction of monomers to form long polymer chains, often yielding higher melting points and improved mechanical properties. In contrast, the ring-opening polymerization method utilizes cyclic monomers, resulting in lower molecular weights and distinct characteristics suitable for specific applications. Each method influences the final product's properties, making them suitable for various industries, including automotive and textiles.</p></p>
