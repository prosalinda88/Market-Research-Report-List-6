<p><p>Aviation actuator systems are essential components in aircraft for controlling various functions such as flight surfaces and landing gear. There are three main types of actuator systems: Electromechanical, which use electric motors; Electro Hydraulic, which combine electric and hydraulic power; and Electro Hydrostatic, which use a combination of electric, hydraulic, and hydrostatic power. Each type offers specific advantages and is used based on the specific requirements of the aircraft and its systems. These actuator systems play a crucial role in ensuring the safe and efficient operation of aircraft.</p></p>
