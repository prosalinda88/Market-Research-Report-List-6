<p><p>The common mode inductors market is primarily segmented into SMD (Surface Mount Device) common mode choke coils and through-hole common mode choke coils. SMD choke coils are compact and ideal for automated assembly, making them suitable for high-density circuit applications. In contrast, through-hole choke coils are typically used in applications requiring higher power handling and durability due to their robust design. Both types serve to filter out unwanted noise and improve the electromagnetic compatibility of electronic devices.</p></p>
