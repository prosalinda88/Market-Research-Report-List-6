<p><p>IC lead frames are an essential component in the semiconductor industry and are categorized into two main types: Stamping Process Lead Frame and Etching Process Lead Frame. Stamping Process Lead Frames are produced by stamping metal sheets to create the desired shape and features, while Etching Process Lead Frames are created through chemical etching of metal sheets. Both types of lead frames play a crucial role in providing structural support and electrical connections for integrated circuits, ensuring proper functionality and performance in various electronic devices.</p></p>
