<p><strong>The OBU (On Board Unit) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sngle-chip OBU (Without IC Card Interface)</li><li>Double-chip OBU (With IC Card Interface)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>On Board Units (OBUs) are critical components in electronic toll collection and vehicle identification systems. The Single-chip OBU, lacking an IC card interface, integrates functions onto a single chip, simplifying installation and reducing costs. In contrast, the Double-chip OBU features an IC card interface, allowing for enhanced communication and additional functionalities, such as user identification and payment options. Both market types cater to varying customer needs and infrastructure requirements, influencing their adoption in transportation systems.</p></p>
