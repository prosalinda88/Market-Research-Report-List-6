<p><p>Electric two-wheeler sharing markets can be categorized into dockless and dock-based systems. Dock-based systems, on the other hand, require users to pick up and return vehicles to designated docking stations. Dockless systems offer more flexibility and convenience for users, while dock-based systems provide a more organized and controlled infrastructure for managing vehicles. Both types cater to the growing demand for eco-friendly urban transportation options.</p></p>
