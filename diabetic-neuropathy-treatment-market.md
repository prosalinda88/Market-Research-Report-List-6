<p><ul><li>Peripheral Neuropathy</li><li>Autonomic Neuropathy</li><li>Proximal Neuropathy</li><li>Focal Neuropathy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The diabetic neuropathy treatment market is categorized into four main types: peripheral neuropathy, autonomic neuropathy, proximal neuropathy, and focal neuropathy. Peripheral neuropathy affects limbs and can cause pain and numbness. Autonomic neuropathy impacts involuntary functions like digestion and heart rate. Proximal neuropathy typically affects the thighs and hips, leading to weakness, while focal neuropathy causes sudden weakness or pain in specific nerves, often in the face or torso. Each type requires tailored management strategies to alleviate symptoms and improve quality of life.</p></p>
