<p><p>Anodized Aluminium Market consists of two main types - Nanoporous Type and Porous Type. Nanoporous Type refers to aluminum that has been anodized to create a highly ordered and controlled nanoporous structure on its surface. This type is used in applications such as sensors and catalyst supports. Porous Type, on the other hand, is anodized aluminum with a more random and uncontrolled porous structure. This type is commonly used in architectural applications for its aesthetic appeal and corrosion resistance.</p></p>
