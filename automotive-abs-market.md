<p><p>The automotive ABS (Anti-lock Braking System) market is categorized into three main types based on channel configuration. The one-channel type features a single sensor for one wheel, ideal for smaller vehicles. The three-channel type has sensors for front wheels and one rear wheel, offering improved control and performance. The four-channel type includes individual sensors for all four wheels, providing maximum stability and safety, making it suitable for high-performance and luxury vehicles. Each type influences the overall braking efficiency and vehicle safety.</p></p>
