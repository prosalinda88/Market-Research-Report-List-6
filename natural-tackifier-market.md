<p><p>Natural tackifiers are additives that help improve the adhesive properties of a substance. There are several types of natural tackifiers such as starch, xanthan gum, natural rubber, agar, and other derivatives. Starch-based tackifiers are derived from plants and are biodegradable. Xanthan gum is a natural polysaccharide that helps improve the viscosity and stability of adhesives. Natural rubber tackifiers are obtained from rubber tree sap and are known for their strong adhesive properties. Agar is a natural seaweed-derived tackifier with excellent gelling and thickening properties. Other natural tackifiers include plant-based resins and gums.</p></p>
