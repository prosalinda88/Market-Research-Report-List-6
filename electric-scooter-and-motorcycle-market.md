<p><p>The electric scooter and motorcycle market consists of two main types of batteries: Sealed Lead Acid (SLA) and Lithium Ion (Li-Ion). SLA batteries are more affordable and have a longer lifespan, but are heavier and bulkier. Li-Ion batteries, on the other hand, are lighter, smaller, and have a higher energy density, but are more expensive. In the market, SLA batteries are commonly used in entry-level or budget-friendly electric scooters and motorcycles, while Li-Ion batteries are typically found in high-end models with superior performance and technology.</p></p>
