<p><ul><li>Micro-Porous Hygiene Films</li><li>Non-Porous Hygiene Films</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hygiene films market is categorized into micro-porous and non-porous types. Micro-porous hygiene films allow moisture vapor to escape while preventing liquid penetration, making them suitable for breathable products like adult diapers and feminine hygiene items. Non-porous hygiene films, on the other hand, create a barrier against liquids and contaminants, ensuring higher protection levels for applications like medical and surgical products. Each type caters to different needs in hygiene, comfort, and functionality across various consumer segments.</p></p>
