<p><strong>The UV-Vis Spectrophotometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Beam UV-Vis Spectrophotometers</li><li>Double-Beam UV-Vis Spectrophotometers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UV-Vis spectrophotometers market is segmented into single-beam and double-beam types. Single-beam UV-Vis spectrophotometers measure sample absorbance by analyzing light before and after passing through the sample, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness. In contrast, double-beam UV-Vis spectrophotometers utilize two beams of light—one for the sample and one for a reference—enhancing accuracy and allowing for real-time compensation of fluctuations in light source intensity. These features make double-beam models preferred for more precise applications in various industries.</p></p>
