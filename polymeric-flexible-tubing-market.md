<p><p>The polymeric flexible tubing market is categorized into three main types: thermosetting elastomers, thermoplastic resins (non-elastomeric), and thermoplastic elastomers (TPE). Thermosetting elastomers are durable and resistant to extreme temperatures, often used in applications requiring robust performance. Thermoplastic resins, while not inherently elastic, offer rigidity and excellent chemical resistance. Thermoplastic elastomers combine the flexibility of elastomers with the processability of thermoplastics, making them versatile for a variety of applications, including automotive, medical, and industrial uses.</p></p>
