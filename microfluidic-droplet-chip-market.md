<p><strong>The Microfluidic Droplet Chip Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive</li><li>Active</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The microfluidic droplet chip market is divided into two main types: passive and active. Passive microfluidic chips rely on surface interactions and gravity to manipulate fluid flow without external forces, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness. In contrast, active microfluidic chips utilize external energy sources, such as electric fields or pressure, to control droplet movement and behavior with greater precision. Both types cater to various applications, including diagnostics, drug delivery, and biochemical analysis, each with unique advantages based on the required performance and complexity.</p></p>
