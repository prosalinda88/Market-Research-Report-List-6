<p><strong>The Medical Compression Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dynamic</li><li>Static</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical compression pump market is categorized into dynamic and static segments. Dynamic compression pumps utilize a cycled pressure system to promote blood circulation, often used in conditions like chronic venous insufficiency or lymphedema. These pumps actively vary pressure to enhance fluid movement. In contrast, static compression pumps maintain a consistent pressure level to provide support without active cycling. Both types aim to improve venous return and reduce swelling, catering to different patient needs and clinical scenarios.</p></p>
