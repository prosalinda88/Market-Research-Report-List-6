<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The OEM Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Powder Coatings</li><li>Water-borne Coatings</li><li>Solvent-borne Coatings</li><li>Radiation Curable Coatings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The OEM coatings market includes several types: </p><p>1. **Powder Coatings** are dry powders that provide a durable finish, often used in automotive and appliance applications.  </p><p>2. **Water-borne Coatings** utilize water as a solvent, offering eco-friendly options with reduced VOC emissions.  </p><p>3. **Solvent-borne Coatings** employ organic solvents for application, delivering excellent adhesion and finish but with higher VOCs.  </p><p>4. **Radiation Curable Coatings** cure quickly upon exposure to UV light or electron beams, providing rapid processing and strong performance. Each type serves specific applications across industries.</p></p>
