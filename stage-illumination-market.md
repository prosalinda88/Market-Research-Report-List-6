<p><p>Stage illumination encompasses various lighting technologies, primarily LED, halogen, and discharge lamps. LED lights are energy-efficient, long-lasting, and capable of vibrant color rendering, making them popular for dynamic performances. Halogen lamps offer bright, consistent lighting but consume more energy and have a shorter lifespan. Discharge lamps, including metal halide and xenon, provide powerful illumination and are ideal for large venues, but they require more time to warm up. Each type serves unique requirements in staging and performance aesthetics.</p></p>
