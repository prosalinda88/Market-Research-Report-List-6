<p><p>Waterproof Breathable Textiles (WBT) are designed to prevent water penetration while allowing moisture vapor to escape, essential for outdoor and active wear. The market primarily includes PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene) and EPFE (Expanded Polytetrafluoroethylene) textiles. PTFE textiles offer excellent waterproofing and breathability, widely used in high-performance apparel and outdoor gear. EPFE, with a more open structure, enhances breathability and is favored in applications requiring higher air permeability, such as in sportswear and technical clothing, ensuring comfort in varied conditions.</p></p>
