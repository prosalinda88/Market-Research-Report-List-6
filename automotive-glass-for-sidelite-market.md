<p><p>The automotive glass sidelites market encompasses three main types: tempered, laminated, and others. Tempered glass is heat-treated for increased strength and safety, shattering into small, blunt pieces upon impact. Laminated glass consists of multiple layers with a plastic interlayer, providing enhanced safety and noise reduction, and preventing shattering. Other types may include acrylic or polycarbonate materials, which offer alternative benefits like reduced weight or increased design flexibility. Each type caters to specific safety, aesthetic, and performance requirements in vehicles.</p></p>
