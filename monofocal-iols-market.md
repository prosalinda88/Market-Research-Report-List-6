<p><p>Monofocal IOLs, or monofocal intraocular lenses, are implants used in cataract surgery to replace the eye's natural lens when it becomes clouded by a cataract. Aspheric Monofocal IOLs are designed to reduce spherical aberration and improve image quality, especially in low light conditions. Spheric Monofocal IOLs have a uniform curvature and provide clear vision at a single focal distance. Both types of IOLs are popular choices for cataract surgery patients, offering improved vision and quality of life. The market for these IOLs continues to grow as advancements in technology lead to better outcomes for patients.</p></p>
