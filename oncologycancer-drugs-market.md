<p><p>Oncology/Cancer drugs market can be categorized into various types based on their mechanisms of action. Antimetabolite drugs interfere with the DNA synthesis process in cancer cells. Alkaloid drugs are derived from plants and have cytotoxic effects on cancer cells. Hormone drugs target cancers that are hormone-sensitive. Targeted drugs work by specifically targeting cancer cells while sparing healthy cells. Platinum-based drugs bind to DNA and prevent cell division in cancer cells. Other markets include immunotherapy, gene therapy, and angiogenesis inhibitors.</p></p>
