<p><p>Automated Compounding Systems are used in pharmacies to precisely measure and mix medications. There are two main types: Gravimetric Automated Compounding Systems and Volumetric Automated Compounding Systems. Gravimetric systems measure ingredients by weight, providing more accurate and consistent results. Volumetric systems, on the other hand, measure ingredients by volume. Each type has its own advantages and is suitable for different applications based on the specific needs of the pharmacy.</p></p>
