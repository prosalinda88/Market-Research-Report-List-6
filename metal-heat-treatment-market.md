<p><ul><li>General Heat Treatment</li><li>Surface Heat Treatment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metal heat treatment market is broadly categorized into two main types: general heat treatment and surface heat treatment. General heat treatment involves processes like annealing, quenching, and tempering, which alter the overall properties of metal components for improved strength and ductility. In contrast, surface heat treatment focuses on enhancing specific surface characteristics, such as hardness and wear resistance, through techniques like case hardening and induction hardening. Both types are vital for optimizing metal performance in various applications.</p></p>
