<p><p>Surgical staplers are divided into two main types: Laparoscopic Staplers and Open Staplers. Laparoscopic Staplers are specifically designed for minimally invasive procedures, allowing for smaller incisions and reduced scarring. They are used for procedures such as cholecystectomies and appendectomies. Open Staplers, on the other hand, are used for traditional open surgeries where a larger incision is made. They are commonly used for procedures such as bowel resections and gastric bypass surgeries. Both types of staplers are essential tools in modern surgical practice.</p></p>
