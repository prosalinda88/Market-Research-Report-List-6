<p><p>The Thermoelectric Generator (TEG) modules market includes two main types: single-stage and multistage modules. Single-stage modules consist of a single thermoelectric element, providing simpler designs and lower costs, making them suitable for low-temperature applications. In contrast, multistage modules utilize multiple thermoelectric elements stacked in series, enabling higher efficiency and power output by effectively harnessing larger temperature differentials. These multistage systems are ideal for industrial and automotive applications where maximizing energy conversion is crucial.</p></p>
