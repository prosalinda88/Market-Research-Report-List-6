<p><ul><li>Saddle</li><li>Waveform</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Silicone elastic gaskets are essential sealing solutions that cater to various applications, primarily categorized into saddle and waveform types. Saddle gaskets feature a U-shaped cross-section, allowing for uniform compression and optimal sealing in joints and flanges, ideal for heavy machinery and automotive applications. Waveform gaskets, with their unique undulating profiles, provide enhanced flexibility and adaptability to uneven surfaces, making them suitable for demanding environments like aerospace and medical devices. Both types ensure reliable sealing performance, contributing to equipment longevity.</p></p>
