<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Fecal Occult Blood Diagnostic Kits Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Immunochemical Fecal Occult Blood Test (iFOBT or FIT)</li><li>Guaiac Fecal Occult Blood Test (gFOBT)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fecal occult blood diagnostic kits market consists primarily of two types: Immunochemical Fecal Occult Blood Test (iFOBT or FIT) and Guaiac Fecal Occult Blood Test (gFOBT). iFOBT detects human hemoglobin in stool using antibodies, offering higher specificity and sensitivity for colorectal cancer screening. gFOBT, on the other hand, is based on the guaiac reaction to detect peroxidase activity from heme in blood, which can lead to more false positives. Both tests aim to aid in early cancer detection and gastrointestinal bleeding assessment.</p></p>
