<p><ul><li>X-Ray Fluorescence Spectrometer</li><li>Molecular Fluorescence Spectrometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fluorescence spectroscopy encompasses various market types, including X-ray fluorescence (XRF) spectrometers and molecular fluorescence spectrometers. XRF spectrometers are utilized for elemental analysis by measuring the secondary X-ray emissions from a sample when excited by X-rays, commonly applied in material science and environmental monitoring. On the other hand, molecular fluorescence spectrometers analyze the fluorescence of molecules after excitation by light, widely used in biological research, drug discovery, and analytical chemistry due to their sensitivity and high resolution.</p></p>
