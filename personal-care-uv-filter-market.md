<p><p>The Personal Care UV Filter market is primarily categorized into two types: organic and inorganic UV filters. Organic UV filters are chemical compounds that absorb UV radiation, offering protection by converting it into heat, which is then released from the skin. Conversely, inorganic UV filters consist of physical minerals like zinc oxide and titanium dioxide, which reflect and scatter UV rays. Both types are widely used in sunscreens and personal care products to safeguard skin from sun damage while providing varying formulations and skin benefits.</p></p>
