<p><ul><li>APP</li><li>SBS</li><li>SEBS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bitumen membranes market consists of various types, including APP (Atactic Polypropylene), SBS (Styrene-Butadiene-Styrene), and SEBS (Styrene-Ethylene-Butylene-Styrene). APP membranes are known for their UV resistance and flexibility, making them suitable for high-temperature applications. SBS membranes offer excellent elasticity and durability, ideal for low-temperature conditions. SEBS membranes combine the advantages of both, providing superior thermal stability and elasticity. These types are crucial for waterproofing and protecting structures in construction and industrial applications.</p></p>
