<p><ul><li>Trivalent OPV</li><li>Bivalent OPV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Oral Polio Vaccines (OPV) are crucial for polio eradication efforts, available in different formulations. The trivalent OPV targets all three poliovirus serotypes (type 1, 2, and 3), while the bivalent OPV focuses on types 1 and 3, excluding type 2, which has been eradicated. The market for these vaccines varies, with trivalent OPV being used in widespread immunization campaigns, while bivalent OPV is increasingly adopted due to its enhanced safety profile and effectiveness against circulating vaccine-derived polioviruses.</p></p>
