<p><p>The Automotive Suspension System Lubricants market includes two main types: lubricating oil for fluid suspension and independent suspension lubricating oil. Lubricating oil for fluid suspension is designed to reduce friction and wear in hydraulic systems, enhancing performance and longevity. Independent suspension lubricating oil, on the other hand, specifically caters to vehicles with independent suspension systems, ensuring smooth operation and stability. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining vehicle handling, ride comfort, and overall suspension efficiency.</p></p>
