<p><strong>The Metallic Flange Gasket Sheet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flat Flange</li><li>Non-planar Flange</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metallic flange gasket sheet market is categorized into two main types: flat flange gaskets and non-planar flange gaskets. Flat flange gaskets are designed for even surfaces, ensuring a tight seal between flat mating surfaces, commonly used in piping applications. In contrast, non-planar flange gaskets accommodate irregular or raised surfaces, providing flexibility for uneven connections. Both types are essential in various industries, such as oil and gas, automotive, and chemical processing, where leak prevention and durability are critical.</p></p>
