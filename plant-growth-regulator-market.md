<p><p>The Plant Growth Regulator (PGR) market encompasses various types, each serving distinct functions in plant development. Cytokinins promote cell division and shoot proliferation, while auxins control elongation and root growth. Gibberellins stimulate seed germination and flowering. Ethylene and ethylene releasers manage ripening and fruit development. Mepiquat chloride is used to regulate plant height and improve crop yield in cotton. Collectively, these regulators enhance agricultural productivity and efficiency by optimizing plant growth processes.</p></p>
