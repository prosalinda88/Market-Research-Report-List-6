<p><p>Disposable centrifuge tubes are available in two main types - round bottom centrifuge tubes and conical centrifuge tubes. Round bottom centrifuge tubes have a rounded bottom shape, while conical centrifuge tubes have a tapered or conical bottom shape. The choice between these two types depends on the specific requirements of the centrifugation process. Round bottom tubes are ideal for pelleting and harvesting applications, while conical tubes are preferred for cell culture and molecular biology applications due to their ability to concentrate samples at the bottom.</p></p>
