<p><p>The sterilization indicators market encompasses various types used to verify sterilization processes. Dry heat sterilization indicators assess the effectiveness of dry heat methods by changing color under specific temperatures. Ethylene oxide sterilization indicators monitor the presence of ethylene oxide gas, signaling successful sterilization. Hydrogen peroxide indicators ensure the efficiency of plasma-based sterilization, while steam sterilization indicators confirm the efficacy of steam sterilizers. Each type serves a crucial role in ensuring safety and compliance in healthcare and laboratory environments.</p></p>
