<p><p>The endoscope reprocessing detergents market is segmented into enzyme detergents and non-enzymatic detergents. Enzyme detergents contain proteases and other enzymes that effectively break down organic matter, facilitating the cleaning of endoscopes by removing biological contaminants. In contrast, non-enzymatic detergents rely on their chemical composition for cleaning, making them suitable for non-organic residues. Both types are essential in ensuring proper sterilization and maintenance of endoscopes, promoting patient safety and infection prevention in healthcare settings.</p></p>
