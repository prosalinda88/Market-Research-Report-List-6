<p><p>The cosmetic grade kaolin clay market consists of three main types: hard kaolin, soft kaolin, and sandy kaolin. Hard kaolin is known for its fine particle size and suitability in formulations requiring higher plasticity and strength. Soft kaolin is more absorbent and has a silky texture, making it ideal for facial masks and powders. Sandy kaolin, characterized by larger particle sizes, is often used for exfoliating products, providing gentle scrubbing action while retaining important moisture content.</p></p>
