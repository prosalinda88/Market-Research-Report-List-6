<p><strong>The Wafer Tape Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tape for Grinding</li><li>Tape for Dicing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wafer tape market comprises two primary types: tape for grinding and tape for dicing. Tape for grinding is used to secure wafers during the grinding process, ensuring stability and precision while reducing surface defects. In contrast, tape for dicing is specifically designed to hold wafers in place during the cutting process into individual chips, providing support and minimizing damage. Both types are essential in semiconductor manufacturing, optimizing efficiency and product quality.</p></p>
