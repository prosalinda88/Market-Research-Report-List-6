<p><p>The ETFE tubing market comprises two primary types: heat shrinkable tubing and extruded tubing. Heat shrinkable tubing is designed to shrink in diameter when heat is applied, providing a tight, secure fit for insulation and protection. In contrast, extruded tubing is manufactured through a continuous process, resulting in uniformity and flexibility, suitable for various applications such as wiring and industrial uses. Both types are valued for their excellent chemical resistance, lightweight properties, and durability in demanding environments.</p></p>
