<p><ul><li>Addition Type</li><li>Condensation Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Two-Component RTV (Room Temperature Vulcanizing) Rubber market can be divided into two main types: Addition Type and Condensation Type. Addition Type RTV utilizes catalysts to promote polymerization without by-products, resulting in lower shrinkage and environmentally friendly processes. It is favored for precise applications. In contrast, Condensation Type RTV involves a chemical reaction that releases small by-products, often leading to slight shrinkage. This type is generally cost-effective and suitable for general sealing and bonding applications.</p></p>
