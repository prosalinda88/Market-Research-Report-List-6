<p><p>Blind Spot Object Detection System Market includes both Automatic and Manual types. Automatic systems use sensors and cameras to detect objects in the blind spot of a vehicle and provide warnings to the driver. These systems require minimal human intervention and are more reliable. On the other hand, Manual systems rely on the driver to visually check blind spots using mirrors and turning their head. While Manual systems are cost-effective, they are also more prone to human error and may not always detect objects accurately.</p></p>
