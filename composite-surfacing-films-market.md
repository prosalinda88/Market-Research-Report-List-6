<p><p>The Composite Surfacing Films market is segmented into two primary types based on processing methods: Autoclave Processing and Out-Of-Autoclave Processing. Autoclave Processing involves curing composite materials under elevated temperature and pressure, ensuring superior mechanical properties and surface finish. In contrast, Out-Of-Autoclave Processing utilizes alternative curing methods, typically at atmospheric pressure, offering greater flexibility and cost-effectiveness. Both methods are essential in aerospace, automotive, and other industries, catering to varying project requirements and production volumes while maintaining quality.</p></p>
