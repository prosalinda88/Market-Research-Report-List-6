<p><p>The cardiac pacing market encompasses various types of devices tailored for specific patient needs. Temporary cardiac pacing provides short-term support, while single-chamber pacing targets either the atrium or ventricle. Dual-chamber pacing synchronizes both chambers for improved heart function. Single-chamber ICD and dual-chamber ICD devices offer defibrillation and pacing for arrhythmias. Three-chamber CRT-P devices enhance heart efficiency in heart failure patients, while three-chamber CRT-D is equipped with defibrillation capabilities, providing comprehensive therapy for advanced heart conditions.</p></p>
