<p><ul><li>V-FFS</li><li>H-FFS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bagging machines for the solid market are designed to efficiently package bulk materials into bags. The V-FFS (Vertical Form Fill Seal) type forms bags from a flat roll of film, fills them with the product, and seals them vertically, making it ideal for flowable solids. In contrast, the H-FFS (Horizontal Form Fill Seal) operates horizontally, accommodating larger or bulkier items. Both systems enhance packaging speed and consistency while minimizing manual handling, catering to diverse industries such as food and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
