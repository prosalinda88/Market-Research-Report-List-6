<p><ul><li>Twisted Pair(Copper)</li><li>Coax(Copper)</li><li>Optic Cables(Fibre)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The signal data cables market encompasses three primary types: twisted pair cables, coaxial cables, and optical fiber cables. Twisted pair cables, primarily copper, are widely used for data transmission in local area networks due to their cost-effectiveness and ease of installation. Coaxial cables, also copper-based, provide robust signal quality for television and internet services. Optical fiber cables utilize glass fibers to transmit data at higher speeds and longer distances, making them ideal for high-bandwidth applications and telecommunication networks.</p></p>
