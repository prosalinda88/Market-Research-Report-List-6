<p><p>Resin additives are used to enhance the properties of resins during processing and end-use. Fillers are added to improve strength and stiffness, while colorants enhance appearance. Plasticizers increase flexibility and impact resistance, while antioxidants prevent degradation. Stabilizers improve weatherability and thermal stability. Other additives may include flame retardants, lubricants, and reinforcing agents. Each type of resin additive serves a specific purpose in enhancing the performance and durability of the resin in various applications.</p></p>
