<p><strong>The Aesthetic Orthodontic Bracket Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional Ligation</li><li>Self-Ligating Brackets</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aesthetic orthodontic bracket market is categorized primarily into conventional ligation and self-ligating brackets. Conventional ligation brackets utilize elastic ties or metal ligatures to secure the archwire, offering effective tooth movement but requiring more frequent adjustments. In contrast, self-ligating brackets feature a built-in mechanism that holds the archwire in place, reducing friction and allowing for smoother tooth movement. This design often results in shorter treatment times and fewer orthodontist visits, appealing to patients seeking efficient and comfortable orthodontic solutions.</p></p>
