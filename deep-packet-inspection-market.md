<p><strong>The Deep Packet Inspection Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Standalone DPI</li><li>Integrated DPI</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Deep Packet Inspection (DPI) technology is used for examining data packets in network traffic. Standalone DPI refers to dedicated devices solely focused on packet inspection, offering high performance and scalability for network security and management. Integrated DPI, on the other hand, is embedded within other networking devices like routers and firewalls, providing DPI capabilities along with other functionalities. Both types cater to different market needs, with standalone DPI being preferred for specialized use cases and intensive packet analysis, while integrated DPI offers convenience and cost-effectiveness for general networking purposes.</p></p>
