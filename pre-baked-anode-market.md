<p><p>Pre-Baked Anode Market Types CA-I, CA-II, and CA-III refer to different grades of pre-baked carbon anodes used in the aluminum industry. CA-I anodes are high quality and provide good performance, suitable for high-current applications. CA-II anodes are of medium quality and are often used in smelters with lower operating currents. CA-III anodes are lower quality and are typically used in smelters with the lowest operating currents. Each type of pre-baked anode is designed to suit specific requirements in the aluminum production process.</p></p>
