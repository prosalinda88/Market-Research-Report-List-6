<p><p>Sludge dewatering systems can be broadly categorized into mobile and static types. Mobile dewatering systems are designed for flexibility and portability, allowing them to be transported to different locations for on-site sludge treatment. These systems are often compact and suitable for temporary or variable sludge generation sites. In contrast, static dewatering systems are fixed installations that provide a consistent and continuous dewatering process over time. They are ideal for facilities with stable, high-volume sludge production, offering efficiency and reliability in operations.</p></p>
