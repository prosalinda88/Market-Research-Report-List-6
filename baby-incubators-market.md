<p><p>The baby incubator market comprises two main types: transport baby incubators and normal baby incubators. Transport baby incubators are designed for safe and stable transport of premature or critically ill infants, often featuring lightweight, portable designs with essential life-support systems. In contrast, normal baby incubators are primarily intended for use in hospitals, providing a controlled environment to support the growth and development of newborns. Both types aim to ensure the health and safety of vulnerable infants in different care settings.</p></p>
