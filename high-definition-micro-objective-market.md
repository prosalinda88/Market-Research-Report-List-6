<p><p>The high-definition micro objective market encompasses three primary types based on magnification power: low power objective, medium power objective, and high power objective. Low power objectives typically offer a magnification range of 4x to 10x, suitable for initial specimen examination. Medium power objectives, ranging from 10x to 40x, provide greater detail for cellular structures. High power objectives, usually 40x to 100x, are designed for intricate observations, such as fine cellular components, making them essential in advanced microscopy applications.</p></p>
