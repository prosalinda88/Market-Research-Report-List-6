Metal substrates, often aluminum or copper, offer enhanced thermal conductivity, ideal for high-power devices. Ceramic substrates provide high thermal and electrical insulation, making them suitable for high-frequency applications. Each type serves specific needs in the electronics industry, enhancing performance and reliability.</p></p>
