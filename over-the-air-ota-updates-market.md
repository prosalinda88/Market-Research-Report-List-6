<p><p>Over the Air (OTA) updates are essential for maintaining and enhancing electronic devices through remote updates. Firmware over-the-air (FOTA) focuses on upgrading the low-level software that controls hardware functions, ensuring optimal performance and security. Software over-the-air (SOTA) updates, on the other hand, pertain to application software enhancements or system upgrades, improving user experience and adding new features. Both market types play a crucial role in the lifecycle management of connected devices, especially in industries such as automotive and consumer electronics.</p></p>
