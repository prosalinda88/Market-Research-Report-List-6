<p><p>Veneer-faced panels for flooring, typically made from engineered wood products, come primarily in two types: particleboard faced and MDF (medium-density fiberboard) faced. Particleboard faced panels offer a cost-effective solution with decent stability and strength, making them suitable for various applications. In contrast, MDF faced panels provide a smoother surface and better density, resulting in enhanced durability and a finer finish. Both types are designed to support decorative veneers, providing aesthetic appeal while ensuring structural integrity in flooring systems.</p></p>
