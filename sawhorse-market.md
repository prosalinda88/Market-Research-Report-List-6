<p><p>Sawhorse markets can be categorized into two main types: foldable and not foldable. Foldable sawhorses are designed for easy storage and portability, featuring hinges that allow them to collapse flat when not in use. This makes them ideal for mobile workspaces and projects where space is limited. In contrast, not foldable sawhorses are sturdier with a fixed design, offering greater stability for heavy-duty tasks but requiring more space for storage. Each type serves specific needs based on mobility and stability requirements.</p></p>
