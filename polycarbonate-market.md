<p><p>The polycarbonate market is primarily divided into two categories: Bisphenol A (BPA) aromatic polycarbonate and engineering plastic glass fiber reinforced polycarbonate. BPA aromatic polycarbonate is known for its transparency, impact resistance, and thermal stability, making it ideal for applications such as eyewear lenses and safety shields. On the other hand, engineering plastic glass fiber reinforced polycarbonate offers enhanced strength and rigidity, making it suitable for demanding applications in automotive and construction industries. Both types serve diverse industrial needs effectively.</p></p>
