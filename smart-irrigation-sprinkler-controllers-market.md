<p><p>The smart irrigation sprinkler controllers market primarily offers two types: weather-based controllers and sensor-based controllers. Weather-based controllers utilize real-time weather data, adjusting watering schedules based on temperature, humidity, and rainfall forecasts, ensuring efficient water usage. In contrast, sensor-based controllers rely on soil moisture sensors and other environmental indicators to determine when and how much to water, promoting optimal plant health. Both types enhance irrigation efficiency, reduce water waste, and support sustainable landscape management practices.</p></p>
