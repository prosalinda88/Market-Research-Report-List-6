<p><strong>The Vacuum Pumps for Medical Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Displacement</li><li>Centrifugal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vacuum pumps are important in the medical field for various applications. Two main types are Positive Displacement and Centrifugal pumps. Positive Displacement pumps work by trapping a fixed amount of gas and then forcing it out to create a vacuum, making them ideal for use in medical equipment like dialysis machines. On the other hand, Centrifugal pumps rely on the rotation of an impeller to create suction, making them suitable for applications in medical laboratories and vacuum sealers. Both types play important roles in ensuring effective and reliable medical operations.</p></p>
