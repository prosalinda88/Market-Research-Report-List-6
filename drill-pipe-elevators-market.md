<p><p>Drill pipe elevators are categorized into three main types based on their design and functionality: side door, closed loop, and others. Side door elevators have a hinged side door for easy placement and removal of the drill pipe. Closed loop elevators feature a continuous circular design for secure gripping of the pipe. The "others" category includes various elevator designs not falling under the side door or closed loop types. Each type offers unique benefits and is suited for specific drilling operations.</p></p>
