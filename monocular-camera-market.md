<p><strong>The Monocular Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Night Vision</li><li>Thermal imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The monocular camera market is divided primarily into night vision and thermal imaging types. Night vision cameras amplify low-light conditions, making them suitable for surveillance and outdoor activities in darkness. In contrast, thermal imaging cameras detect heat emitted by objects, allowing for visibility in complete darkness and through obscurants like fog or smoke. Both technologies serve critical roles in security, military operations, wildlife observation, and search and rescue missions, each offering unique advantages depending on the application environment.</p></p>
