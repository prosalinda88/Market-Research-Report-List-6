<p><p>The Powertrain Heat Exchanger market includes various types essential for thermal management in vehicles. Radiators dissipate engine heat, maintaining optimal operating temperatures. Intercoolers cool intake air, enhancing engine performance and efficiency. Oil coolers regulate the temperature of engine and transmission oil, preventing overheating and extending component life. EGR (Exhaust Gas Recirculation) coolers lower exhaust gas temperatures, reducing emissions and improving fuel efficiency. Together, these components contribute to improved vehicle performance, reliability, and environmental compliance.</p></p>
