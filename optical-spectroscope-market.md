<p><p>An Optical Spectroscope is a device used to analyze the properties of light. There are two main types of optical spectroscopes: Visible Optic and Invisible Optic. </p><p>Visible Optic spectroscopes are designed to analyze light that is within the visible spectrum, which is the range of wavelengths that the human eye can perceive. </p><p>Invisible Optic spectroscopes, on the other hand, are used to analyze light that is outside of the visible spectrum, such as ultraviolet or infrared light. These devices are crucial for various scientific and industrial applications where analyzing invisible light is necessary.</p></p>
