<p><p>Rubber injection machines are essential for manufacturing rubber products through precise molding processes. The market is divided into two main types: vertical and horizontal machines. Vertical types are characterized by their upright configuration, which is ideal for small to medium-sized components, allowing for efficient space utilization and easier loading of molds. In contrast, horizontal types offer larger production volumes, accommodating bigger molds and facilitating automated processes, making them suitable for large-scale operations. Both types cater to varying industry demands.</p></p>
