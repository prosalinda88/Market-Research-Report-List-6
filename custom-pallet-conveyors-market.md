<p><ul><li>Drag Chain</li><li>Roller (driven or gravity)</li><li>Plastic Type</li><li>Slat Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Custom pallet conveyors come in various types based on the design and functionality. Drag chain conveyors use chains to pull pallets along the conveyor. Roller conveyors, either driven by a motor or relying on gravity, use rollers to move pallets. Plastic conveyors are made of durable plastic materials. Slat conveyors use slats to create a sturdy surface for pallets to move. Each type of conveyor offers unique benefits and features to cater to different industries and applications in material handling.</p></p>
