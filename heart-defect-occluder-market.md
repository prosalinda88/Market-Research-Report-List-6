<p><p>The heart defect occluder market encompasses devices designed to treat congenital heart defects like Patent Ductus Arteriosus (PDA), Atrial Septal Defect (ASD), and Ventricular Septal Defect (VSD). PDA occluders close a persistent duct connecting the aorta and pulmonary artery, preventing abnormal blood flow. ASD occluders seal openings between the heart’s upper chambers to improve oxygen-rich blood flow. VSD occluders are used to block holes between the heart's lower chambers, reducing the risk of heart failure and other complications.</p></p>
