<p><p>The desktop electronic nose market comprises several key types of technologies. Metal Oxide Semiconductor (MOS) sensors detect volatile compounds through changes in resistance due to gas adsorption. Conducting Polymers (CP) sensors utilize polymer films that change electrical conductivity in response to chemical interactions. Quartz Crystal Microbalance (QCM) sensors measure mass changes on a quartz crystal surface, offering high sensitivity. Other market technologies may include electrochemical sensors and photoionization detectors, each catering to specific applications in gas detection and analysis.</p></p>
