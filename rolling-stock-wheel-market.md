<p><strong>The Rolling Stock Wheel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Casting</li><li>Forged</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rolling stock wheel market can be divided into two main types - casting and forged. Casting wheels are made by pouring molten metal into a mold, resulting in a more cost-effective but less durable option. On the other hand, forged wheels are created by applying extreme pressure to shape the metal, producing a stronger and more durable wheel, albeit at a higher cost. Both types have their own advantages and disadvantages, and buyers should consider factors such as budget and intended use when choosing between casting and forged wheels.</p></p>
