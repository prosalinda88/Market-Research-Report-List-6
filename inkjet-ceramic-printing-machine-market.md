<p><p>Inkjet Ceramic Printing Machines are categorized into two main types - UV Printing and Dye-sublimation Printing. UV Printing uses ultraviolet light to cure ink on the surface of ceramic materials, resulting in vibrant colors and durable prints. On the other hand, Dye-sublimation Printing involves transferring dye onto ceramics using heat, creating sharp and high-quality images. Both types of printing offer different advantages and are popular choices in the ceramic printing market for their efficiency and versatility.</p></p>
