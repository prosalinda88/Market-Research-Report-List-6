<p><p>The ambulatory cardiac monitoring system market includes several key types. Holter monitors provide continuous ECG recording over 24-48 hours to detect irregularities. Event monitors offer intermittent monitoring, triggered by the patient during symptoms, typically used over longer periods. Mobile cardiac telemetry allows real-time transmission of data to healthcare providers through wireless technology, ensuring continuous observation. Other market segments may include implantable monitors and smartphone-based solutions, catering to diverse patient needs and enhancing cardiac health management.</p></p>
