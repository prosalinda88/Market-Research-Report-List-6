<p><ul><li>Reactive Dye Inks</li><li>Acidic Ink</li><li>Paint Ink</li><li>Dispersion & Sublimation Ink</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital fabric printing ink market comprises several types designed for various fabric applications. Reactive dye inks bond chemically with fibers, offering vibrant colors and durability on cellulose-based fabrics. Acidic inks are suitable for protein fibers like silk and wool, providing brilliant hues. Paint inks deliver versatility across materials, enabling customization for different surfaces. Dispersion inks are ideal for polyester, creating sharp prints, while sublimation inks transfer images onto garments through heat, resulting in vivid, long-lasting designs. Each type serves distinct needs in digital printing.</p></p>
