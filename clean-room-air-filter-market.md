<p><p>The Clean Room Air Filter market primarily features two key types: HEPA (High-Efficiency Particulate Air) filters and ULPA (Ultra-Low Penetration Air) filters. HEPA filters are designed to capture at least 99.97% of particles down to 0.3 microns, making them effective for most clean room applications. ULPA filters offer even higher efficiency, capturing 99.999% of particles as small as 0.12 microns. These filters are essential in environments requiring stringent air quality control, such as pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, and semiconductor manufacturing, ensuring contamination prevention.</p></p>
