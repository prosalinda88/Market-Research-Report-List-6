Nonionic surfactants are versatile and widely used, while anionic surfactants are effective at lowering surface tension. Cationic surfactants are known for their antimicrobial properties, and amphoteric surfactants offer both positive and negative charged groups, providing compatibility with various agrochemical formulations. Each type plays a crucial role in ensuring optimal performance and efficacy in agricultural applications.</p></p>
