<p><strong>The Automotive Components Forging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Closed Die Forgings</li><li>Open Die Forgings</li><li>Rolled Rings Forgings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive components forging market comprises three primary types: closed die forgings, open die forgings, and rolled rings forgings. Closed die forgings involve shaping metal within a die, allowing for intricate designs and high precision; they are commonly used for components like gears and chassis parts. Open die forgings, where metal is shaped between flat dies, are suited for larger, simpler parts such as axles. Rolled rings forgings are specifically used for producing ring-shaped components, ideal for wheels and bearings, offering strength and durability.</p></p>
