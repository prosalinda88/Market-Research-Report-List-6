<p><p>The kinesiology tape market is primarily categorized into two types: roll type and precut type. Roll type consists of long, continuous strips that can be cut to the desired length, allowing for customized application based on individual needs. This flexibility appeals to both professionals and casual users. In contrast, precut type comes in pre-sized strips designed for specific body parts, offering convenience and ease of use, especially for those unfamiliar with taping techniques. Each type serves distinct consumer preferences.</p></p>
