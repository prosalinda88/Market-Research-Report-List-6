<p><p>The Intelligent Digital Sensors Market is categorized into two main types: Insertion Intelligent Digital Sensors and Immersion Intelligent Digital Sensors. Insertion sensors are designed to monitor parameters in pipes or conduits by being inserted directly into the medium, ideal for applications like flow measurement. Immersion sensors, on the other hand, are fully submerged in liquids or environments to detect temperature, pressure, or chemical composition. Both types enhance data accuracy and enable real-time monitoring, driving advancements in industrial automation and process control.</p></p>
