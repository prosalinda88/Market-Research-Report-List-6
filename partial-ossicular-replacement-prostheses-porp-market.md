<p><p>Partial Ossicular Replacement Prostheses (PORP) are medical devices designed to restore hearing in patients with conductive hearing loss due to damage or disease affecting the ossicular chain in the middle ear. These prostheses replace one or more ossicles (the small bones in the middle ear) while preserving the remaining structures to enhance sound transmission to the inner ear.</p><p>The PORP market has been experiencing significant growth, driven by rising prevalence of ear diseases, increasing awareness about hearing impairment, and advancements in medical technology. The demand for minimally invasive surgeries is also propelling market expansion, as PORPs offer less trauma and faster recovery times compared to traditional surgical methods. As aging populations and noise-induced hearing loss cases rise, there is an increasing need for effective hearing solutions.</p><p>The Partial Ossicular Replacement Prostheses PORP Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.40% during the forecast period.
