<p><strong>The Modular Encoder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Incremental Encoders</li><li>Absolute Encoders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The modular encoder market comprises two main types: incremental encoders and absolute encoders. Incremental encoders track position changes by generating signals as the shaft rotates, ideal for applications requiring speed and direction measurement. Absolute encoders provide a unique position value for each shaft angle, ensuring accurate readings even after power loss, making them suitable for applications demanding precise positioning. Both types cater to diverse industries, enhancing automation and control systems with varying precision and reliability needs.</p></p>
