<p><p>The non-pleated air filters market is segmented based on Minimum Efficiency Reporting Value (MERV) ratings, which indicate their filtering efficiency. MERV 3 and below filters primarily capture large particles and are suitable for basic air filtration needs. MERV 4 to 7 filters provide moderate filtration, trapping smaller particles, making them ideal for residential and light commercial use. MERV 8 and above filters offer higher efficiency, effectively capturing finer particles, and are commonly used in hospitals, laboratories, and industrial applications.</p></p>
