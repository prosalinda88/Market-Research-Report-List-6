<p><strong>The Active Wound Care Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dressings</li><li>Grafts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Active wound care market includes dressings and grafts. Dressings are used to cover wounds and promote healing, while grafts are used to replace damaged tissue with healthy tissue. Dressings come in various forms such as foam, hydrogel, alginate, and collagen. Grafts can be synthetic or biological, such as skin grafts or tissue-engineered grafts. Both dressings and grafts play a crucial role in wound management by providing a protective barrier, maintaining moisture, and promoting tissue regeneration.</p></p>
