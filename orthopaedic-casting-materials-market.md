<p><p>The orthopaedic casting materials market comprises three main types: plaster, fiberglass, and others. Plaster casts are traditional, offering excellent moldability and rigidity but are heavier and less water-resistant. Fiberglass casts are lighter, durable, and waterproof, allowing for better patient mobility and comfort. Other materials may include thermoplastics and hybrid options that combine properties of both plaster and fiberglass. Each type serves specific applications based on factors like patient needs, fracture type, and desired recovery outcomes.</p></p>
