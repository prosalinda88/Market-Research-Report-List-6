<p><p>The gene editing tools market includes various technologies such as Zinc Finger Nucleases (ZFNs), TAL Effector Nucleases (TALENs), and the CRISPR-Cas system. ZFNs use engineered zinc finger proteins to target specific DNA sequences, while TALENs utilize transcription activator-like effectors for precision editing. The CRISPR-Cas system, known for its simplicity and efficiency, employs RNA-guided nucleases for targeted modifications. These tools are essential in research, therapeutics, and agriculture, driving advancements in genetic engineering and biotechnology.</p></p>
