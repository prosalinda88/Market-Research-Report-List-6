<p><ul><li>Analog</li><li>Digital</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aerospace tilt sensors market is divided into two main types: analog and digital sensors. Analog tilt sensors measure angular displacement through a continuous voltage output, providing real-time data but potentially limited accuracy in certain conditions. Conversely, digital tilt sensors convert angular measurements into discrete data, offering enhanced precision and easier integration with digital systems. Both types are crucial in aviation and aerospace applications for stabilizing systems, navigation, and monitoring aircraft orientation, ensuring safety and efficiency in operations.</p></p>
