Chromatography instruments are used for separating compounds, allowing for detailed analysis and quantification of drugs. Breath analyzers measure substances present in exhaled breath, commonly used for alcohol detection. Other market types encompass diverse devices and methods, including mass spectrometers and portable testing kits, serving niche applications in drug testing.</p></p>
