<p><p>The dry molasses market comprises three primary types: light molasses, dark molasses, and blackstrap molasses. Light molasses contains a higher sugar content and is often used in food products, while dark molasses has a richer flavor and is suitable for baking and cooking. Blackstrap molasses, the most concentrated and least sweet, is rich in minerals and commonly utilized in animal feed and dietary supplements. Each type serves distinct applications based on sugar content, flavor profile, and nutritional value.</p></p>
