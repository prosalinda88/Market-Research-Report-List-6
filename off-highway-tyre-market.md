<p><p>The off-highway tire market comprises three main types: bias, radial, and solid tires. Bias tires feature layered construction, offering flexibility and durability for rough terrains but less stability. Radial tires, known for their superior tread life and fuel efficiency, have a radial ply structure that enhances performance and comfort. Solid tires, made from solid rubber, provide exceptional puncture resistance and are ideal for demanding environments, such as construction sites, where durability and low maintenance are crucial.</p></p>
