<p><strong>The Water Based Pigments Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Organic Water Based Pigments</li><li>Inorganic Water Based Pigments</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water-based pigments market is categorized into organic and inorganic pigments. Organic water-based pigments, derived from carbon-containing compounds, are known for their vibrant colors and eco-friendly properties, making them suitable for applications in textiles, paints, and coatings. In contrast, inorganic water-based pigments, typically made from minerals, offer excellent stability, opacity, and UV resistance, making them ideal for industrial applications. Both types are increasingly favored due to their reduced environmental impact compared to solvent-based alternatives.</p></p>
