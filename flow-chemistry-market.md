<p><ul><li>Continuous Stirred Tank Reactors (CSTR)</li><li>Plug Flow Reactors (PFR)</li><li>Micro Reactor Systems (MRT)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flow chemistry market encompasses various reactor types, each suited for specific applications. Continuous Stirred Tank Reactors (CSTR) facilitate uniform mixing and consistent product quality, ideal for slower reactions. Plug Flow Reactors (PFR) ensure high conversion rates by allowing reactants to move through the reactor in a "plug" flow, enhancing efficiency. Micro Reactor Systems (MRT) enable precise control over reaction conditions on a small scale, promoting rapid heat and mass transfer. Other reactors include batch and fixed-bed systems, expanding process versatility.</p></p>
