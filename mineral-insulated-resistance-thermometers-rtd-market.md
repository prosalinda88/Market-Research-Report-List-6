<p><p>Mineral Insulated Resistance Thermometers (RTDs) are precision temperature sensors that utilize a metal wire, usually platinum, insulated by a ceramic material. In the market, they are categorized into two types: Simplex Conductor and Duplex Conductor. Simplex conductors feature a single sensing element, offering basic temperature measurements, while duplex conductors include two sensing elements, providing redundancy and improved accuracy. Duplex models are ideal for critical applications where continuous monitoring and reliability are paramount, enhancing operational safety and efficiency.</p></p>
