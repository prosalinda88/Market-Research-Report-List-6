<p><strong>The Cellulose Textile Dyes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct</li><li>Reactive</li><li>VAT</li><li>Basic</li><li>Acid</li><li>Disperse</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cellulose textile dyes market encompasses several types, each with distinct characteristics and applications. Direct dyes offer straightforward application and vibrant colors but have lower wash fastness. Reactive dyes form covalent bonds with cellulose, ensuring durability and vibrancy. VAT dyes are insoluble and require reduction for application, suitable for achieving deep shades. Basic dyes are cationic and ideal for synthetic fibers but less effective on cellulose. Acid dyes work well on wool and silk, while disperse dyes are used for synthetic fibers. Other dyes may include specialty formulations tailored for specific textile needs.</p></p>
