<p><strong>The Commercial Vehicle Thermostat Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Insert Thermostat</li><li>Housing Thermostat</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial vehicle thermostat market encompasses various types, including insert thermostats and housing thermostats. Insert thermostats function as standalone components, directly controlling engine temperature by regulating coolant flow, ensuring optimal performance and efficiency. In contrast, housing thermostats are integrated into the engine's thermostat housing, providing a compact design that simplifies installation and maintenance. Both types play vital roles in managing thermal conditions, enhancing engine longevity, fuel efficiency, and overall reliability in commercial vehicles.</p></p>
