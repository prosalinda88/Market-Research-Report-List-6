<p><p>Optical Fiber Composite Phase Conductor (OPPC) is an advanced technology combining optical fibers with electrical conductors, enhancing power transmission and communication capabilities. The Central Tube Structure OPPC features a central tube housing the optical fibers, ensuring optimal protection and performance. In contrast, the Layer Stranding Structure OPPC incorporates multiple layers of conductors and fibers, improving flexibility and strength. Both types serve to modernize electrical infrastructure, facilitating efficient data transmission and power distribution in a single, compact design.</p></p>
