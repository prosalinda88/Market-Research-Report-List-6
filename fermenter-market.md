<p><p>Fermenter market types include Stirred Tank Fermenter, Air-Lift Fermenter, Fluidised Bed Bioreactor, and Bubble Column Fermenter. Stirred Tank Fermenters have a rotating impeller to mix the contents, while Air-Lift Fermenters use rising bubbles to circulate the mixture. Fluidised Bed Bioreactors have solid particles suspended in the liquid for better oxygen transfer, and Bubble Column Fermenters rely on gas bubbles for agitation. Each type offers unique advantages for different fermentation processes, catering to a variety of industries.</p></p>
