<p><p>Neuroregeneration Therapy encompasses three major market types: Peripheral Nervous System Regeneration, Central Nervous System Regeneration, and Inhibition of Axonal Regrowth. Peripheral Nervous System Regeneration focuses on repairing damaged nerves outside of the brain and spinal cord. Central Nervous System Regeneration targets the repair of nerves within the brain and spinal cord. Inhibition of Axonal Regrowth involves preventing the regrowth of nerve fibers in order to control conditions such as chronic pain or certain neurological disorders.</p></p>
