<p><p>The paper coating materials market includes various types such as GCC (Ground Calcium Carbonate) and PCC (Precipitated Calcium Carbonate), which enhance brightness and opacity. Kaolin clay provides smoothness and printability, while talc improves ink adhesion and surface properties. Starch serves as a binder, enhancing coating performance. SB latex offers flexibility and water resistance, whereas titanium dioxide is crucial for whiteness and opacity. Wax is used for water repellency and surface protection, improving durability and aesthetics in coated paper products.</p></p>
