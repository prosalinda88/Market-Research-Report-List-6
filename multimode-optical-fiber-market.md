The numbers in these types refer to the diameter of the core and cladding in micrometers. The 50/125μm is known for its higher bandwidth and faster data transmission over short distances, while the 62.5/125μm is more commonly used for older systems at lower transmission speeds. Both types have their own advantages and applications in the telecommunications industry.</p></p>
