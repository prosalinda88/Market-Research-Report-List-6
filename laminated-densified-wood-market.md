<p><p>Laminated Densified Wood is available in two main types: Fully Impregnated and Non-Impregnated. This type of wood is ideal for applications where high performance is required, such as construction and electrical insulation. Non-Impregnated Densified Laminated Wood, on the other hand, does not have the resin treatment but still offers increased strength and toughness compared to traditional wood, making it suitable for various industrial uses.</p></p>
