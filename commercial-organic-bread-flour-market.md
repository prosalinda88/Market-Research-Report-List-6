<p><p>Commercial organic bread flour is available in two main market types: Machine Mill Flour and Stone Mill Flour. Machine Mill Flour is produced using automated machinery that grinds and processes the wheat into flour. It is known for its consistent quality and texture. On the other hand, Stone Mill Flour is produced using traditional stone grinding methods, which are believed to preserve more of the wheat's nutrients and flavor. It is often favored by those seeking a more rustic and artisanal touch to their breads.</p></p>
