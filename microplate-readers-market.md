<p><p>Microplate readers are widely used in research laboratories for various applications such as enzyme-linked immunosorbent assays and drug discovery. These readers can be classified into two main types: Optical Filter Microplate Readers and Optical Grating Microplate Readers. Optical Filter Microplate Readers utilize a fixed set of filters to select specific wavelengths of light, while Optical Grating Microplate Readers use diffraction gratings to select wavelengths. Both types offer high sensitivity and accuracy, making them essential tools for analyzing and quantifying samples in a microplate format.</p></p>
