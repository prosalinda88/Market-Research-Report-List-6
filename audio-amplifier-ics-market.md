<p><strong>The Audio Amplifier ICs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Mount</li><li>Through Hole</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The audio amplifier IC market can be categorized into two primary types: surface mount and through-hole. Surface mount audio amplifier ICs are designed for compact circuit boards, offering a space-efficient solution ideal for modern electronic devices. They allow for automated assembly and improved performance due to shorter signal paths. In contrast, through-hole audio amplifier ICs involve leads that insert into drilled holes on PCBs, providing robust mechanical support and ease of handling, making them suitable for prototyping and repairs.</p></p>
