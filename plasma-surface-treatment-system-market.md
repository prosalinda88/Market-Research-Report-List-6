<p><p>Plasma Surface Treatment Systems can be classified into two main types based on pressure levels: Atmospheric Pressure Type and Low Pressure / Vacuum Plasma Type. Atmospheric Pressure Type systems operate at ambient pressure levels, making them suitable for treating larger surfaces at a faster pace. On the other hand, Low Pressure / Vacuum Plasma Type systems operate at reduced pressures, enabling more controlled and targeted treatment of surfaces, ideal for applications requiring precision and customizability. Both types offer unique advantages depending on the specific surface treatment requirements.</p></p>
