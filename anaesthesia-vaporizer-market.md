<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Anaesthesia Vaporizer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plenum Vaporizers</li><li>Drawover Vaporizers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The anaesthesia vaporizer market comprises different types, notably plenum vaporizers and drawover vaporizers. Plenum vaporizers mix anaesthetic agents with fresh gas in a controlled chamber, ensuring consistent delivery during mechanical ventilation. In contrast, drawover vaporizers utilize the patient’s own respiratory effort to draw fresh gas through the vaporizer, suitable for low-flow anaesthesia techniques. Each type offers unique benefits tailored to specific clinical needs, enhancing patient safety and anaesthetic efficiency in various healthcare settings.</p></p>
