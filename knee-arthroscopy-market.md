<p><p>Knee arthroscopy is a minimally invasive surgical procedure used to diagnose and treat various knee joint issues. It involves the insertion of a small camera, known as an arthroscope, through tiny incisions in the knee, allowing surgeons to view the internal structures on a monitor and perform corrective procedures with precision. This technique is often preferred due to its benefits, including reduced recovery time, minimal scarring, and lower rates of infection compared to traditional open surgeries.</p><p>The Knee Arthroscopy Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.40% during the forecast period, driven by an increasing prevalence of knee-related disorders, rising awareness of minimally invasive surgical techniques, and advancements in arthroscopic technologies.
