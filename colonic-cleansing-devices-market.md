<p><p>The colonic cleansing devices market comprises two main types: open system machines and closed system machines. Open system machines utilize a free-flow design, allowing waste to exit into a toilet or receptacle, promoting user comfort and a natural experience. In contrast, closed system machines use a sealed design that recirculates water through a tube, providing greater control and hygiene. Both systems serve the purpose of facilitating detoxification and promoting digestive health, catering to varying user preferences and clinic settings.</p></p>
