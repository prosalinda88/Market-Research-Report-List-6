<p><p>Plasma etching equipment for the solar cell market utilizes various techniques to pattern and remove materials. Sputter etching employs physical bombardment of ions to dislodge atoms from a substrate surface, creating precise patterns. Chemical etching involves reactive gases that chemically alter and remove material, offering greater selectivity and control. Physicochemical etching combines physical and chemical methods, optimizing etching rates and resolutions. Each type serves distinct applications in solar cell manufacturing, enhancing efficiency and performance of photovoltaic devices.</p></p>
