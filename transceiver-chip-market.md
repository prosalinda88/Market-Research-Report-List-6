<p><ul><li>Single-Chip Transceiver</li><li>Standalone-Chip Transceiver</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The transceiver chip market comprises two main types: single-chip transceivers and standalone-chip transceivers. Single-chip transceivers integrate both transmitter and receiver functionalities into a single semiconductor device, optimizing space and cost for applications like consumer electronics and IoT devices. In contrast, standalone-chip transceivers consist of separate transmitter and receiver components, providing greater flexibility and performance for specialized applications, such as telecommunications and industrial systems. Each type caters to different needs in communication technology, balancing integration and modularity.</p></p>
