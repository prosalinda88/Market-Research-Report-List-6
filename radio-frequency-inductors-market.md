<p><p>The Radio Frequency Inductors market features three primary types: Wire Wound, Film, and Multilayer. Wire Wound inductors consist of a coil of wire, offering high inductance and efficiency, suitable for various applications. Film inductors use a thin film of conductive material, providing good performance with lower loss, ideal for high-frequency circuits. Multilayer inductors combine multiple layers of conductive materials to achieve compact designs with high inductance values, often used in portable electronics and RF applications for improved performance and size efficiency.</p></p>
