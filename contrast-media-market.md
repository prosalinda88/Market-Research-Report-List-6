<p><p>The contrast media market is categorized into three main types: X-ray & CT contrast media, MRI contrast media, and ultrasound contrast media. X-ray and CT contrast agents enhance image clarity during scans by improving visibility of internal structures and abnormalities. MRI contrast media, typically gadolinium-based, aids in differentiating tissues for detailed imaging. Ultrasound contrast agents enhance ultrasound images by providing contrast in blood flow and tissue characterization. Each type plays a crucial role in diagnostic imaging across various medical applications.</p></p>
