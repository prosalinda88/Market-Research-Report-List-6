<p><strong>The Nuclear Grade Ion Exchange Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Anion Resin</li><li>Cationic Resin</li><li>Mixed Bed Ion Exchange Resin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nuclear grade ion exchange resin market comprises three main types: anion resins, cationic resins, and mixed bed ion exchange resins. Anion resins selectively remove negatively charged ions, essential for decontaminating water in nuclear facilities. Cationic resins target positively charged ions, playing a crucial role in purifying process waters and wastewater treatment. Mixed bed ion exchange resins combine both anion and cation resins, providing comprehensive water purification for reactor coolant and other critical applications in nuclear technology.</p></p>
