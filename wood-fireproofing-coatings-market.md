<p><strong>The Wood Fireproofing Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-based</li><li>Solvent-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wood fireproofing coatings come in two primary types: water-based and solvent-based. Water-based coatings use water as the primary solvent, offering low odor, easy cleanup, and reduced environmental impact, making them ideal for indoor use. In contrast, solvent-based coatings utilize organic solvents, providing robust adhesion and durability, suited for outdoor applications where moisture and abrasion resistance are critical. Both types aim to enhance fire resistance in wood products while meeting specific performance and application requirements.</p></p>
