<p><p>Far infrared heaters are available in two main types: gas and electric. Gas infrared heaters use propane or natural gas as fuel to generate heat, making them suitable for outdoor spaces or poorly insulated areas. Electric infrared heaters use electricity to produce heat and are commonly used for indoor spaces such as homes, offices, and commercial buildings. Both types offer efficient and cost-effective heating solutions, with gas heaters typically providing higher heat output and electric heaters offering ease of installation and operation.</p></p>
