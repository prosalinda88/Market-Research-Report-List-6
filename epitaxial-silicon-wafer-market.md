<p><p>The epitaxial silicon wafer market comprises several types, each serving distinct applications. Polished wafers have a smooth surface for various semiconductor processes. Epitaxial wafers are grown layers of silicon that enhance device performance. Silicon-On-Insulator (SOI) wafers integrate a silicon layer atop an insulating substrate, improving efficiency in microelectronics. Lapping wafers, used for precision thinning, are processed to achieve specific thicknesses for advanced applications. Together, these types cater to diverse needs in semiconductor fabrication and electronics industries.</p></p>
