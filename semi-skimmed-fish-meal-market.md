<p><strong>The Semi-Skimmed Fish Meal Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The semi-skimmed fish meal market consists of two primary types: Flame Dried (FD) Fish Meal and Steam Dried (SD) Fish Meal. FD Fish Meal is produced using high-temperature drying, resulting in a product with concentrated nutrients but potential nutrient loss due to heat. In contrast, SD Fish Meal employs steam heat to dry the fish, preserving more nutrients and offering a higher-quality product, often preferred for feeding livestock and aquaculture due to its nutritional profile and digestibility.</p></p>
