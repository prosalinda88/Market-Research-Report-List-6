Sacks are commonly used for smaller quantities of chemicals, while drums are preferred for liquid chemicals. FIBCs, also known as bulk bags, are utilized for transporting large volumes of chemicals. Other specialized packaging options may include bottles, containers, or customized packaging solutions tailored to specific chemical products. Each type of packaging offers unique benefits in terms of storage, transportation, and handling of chemicals.</p></p>
