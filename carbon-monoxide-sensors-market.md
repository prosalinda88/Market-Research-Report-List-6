<p><ul><li>Semiconductor</li><li>Infrared</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The carbon monoxide sensors market is primarily divided into two types: semiconductor and infrared sensors. Semiconductor sensors utilize a metal oxide semiconductor to detect CO gas, offering a cost-effective and compact solution, though they may be less selective than other types. In contrast, infrared sensors operate by measuring the absorption of infrared light by carbon monoxide molecules, providing high sensitivity and selectivity. This differentiation allows for various applications, including residential, industrial, and automotive environments, catering to diverse safety needs.</p></p>
