<p><p>Laboratory spectrometer market includes three main types: Mass Spectrometry, Atomic Spectrometry, and Molecular Spectrometry. Mass spectrometry is used to identify and quantify molecules based on their mass-to-charge ratio. Atomic spectrometry analyzes the elemental composition of samples by measuring the absorption or emission of radiation. Molecular spectrometry studies the interaction of molecules with electromagnetic radiation to identify and analyze chemical compounds. These techniques are widely used in various industries such as pharmaceuticals, environmental analysis, and food testing for research and quality control purposes.</p></p>
