<p><p>CMP (Chemical Mechanical Planarization) cleaning solutions are categorized into two market types: acidic and basic. Acidic cleaning solutions typically contain strong acids, effectively removing metal contaminants and oxidized films from semiconductor surfaces. In contrast, basic cleaning solutions utilize strong bases, excelling at dissolving organic residues and particles. Each type serves distinct roles in ensuring the purity and smoothness of wafers during semiconductor manufacturing, with specific formulations tailored to target various types of contaminants for optimal performance in the cleaning process.</p></p>
