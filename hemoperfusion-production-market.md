<p><p>The hemoperfusion production market is categorized into three main types: resin adsorption, activated carbon adsorption, and others. Resin adsorption utilizes synthetic polymers to selectively capture toxins from blood, offering high specificity and efficiency. Activated carbon adsorption employs porous carbon materials to adsorb various impurities, leveraging a large surface area for effective toxin removal. Other methods may include novel materials or technologies, addressing specific therapeutic needs or enhancing performance. Each type plays a crucial role in improving patient outcomes in toxicology and critical care.</p></p>
