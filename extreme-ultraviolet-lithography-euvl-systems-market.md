<p><strong>The Extreme Ultraviolet Lithography (EUVL) Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Laser Produced Plasmas</li><li>Vacuum Sparks</li><li>Gas Discharges</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Extreme Ultraviolet Lithography (EUVL) systems utilize different plasma generation methods to produce the necessary extreme ultraviolet light for advanced semiconductor manufacturing. Laser Produced Plasma (LPP) involves using high-intensity lasers to create plasma and emit EUV light. Vacuum Sparks generate plasma by discharging a high-voltage pulse between electrodes in a vacuum, producing EUV emissions. Gas Discharges create plasma through the ionization of a gas, allowing EUV radiation to be emitted. Each method plays a crucial role in enhancing EUVL technology for cutting-edge chips.</p></p>
