<p><p>The industrial gantry robot market is divided into two main types: open gantry robots and closed gantry robots. Open gantry robots feature a non-enclosed design, allowing for easy access and flexibility in operation, making them ideal for applications with varying workspace requirements. In contrast, closed gantry robots have a more sturdy, enclosed structure, providing enhanced stability and precision for heavy-duty tasks. Both types cater to different industrial needs, from assembly and packaging to material handling and logistics.</p></p>
