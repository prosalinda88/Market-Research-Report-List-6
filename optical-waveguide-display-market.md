<p><strong>The Optical Waveguide Display Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Channel Waveguide</li><li>Planar Waveguide</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical waveguide display market primarily features two types: channel waveguides and planar waveguides. Channel waveguides are designed as narrow, three-dimensional structures that confine light within specific paths, making them ideal for applications requiring precise light manipulation. In contrast, planar waveguides utilize flat surfaces to guide light across larger areas, offering advantages in uniformity and ease of integration into display systems. Both types support advanced display technologies, enhancing visual experiences in consumer electronics and various industrial applications.</p></p>
