In contrast, scissor window regulators employ a scissor-like mechanism for vertical window movement, providing enhanced structural rigidity and reliability. Both types contribute to the convenience and safety of power windows in modern vehicles, catering to consumer preferences for functionality and aesthetics.</p></p>
