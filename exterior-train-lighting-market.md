<p><p>The Exterior Train Lighting market comprises various types, each catering to specific needs. Halogen exterior lighting offers bright illumination with a warm hue but has higher energy consumption. Xenon/HID lighting provides intense brightness and better efficiency than halogens, making it popular in modern trains. Fluorescent lighting is energy-efficient and offers a softer glow, suitable for general lighting purposes. Meanwhile, LED exterior lighting is rapidly gaining traction due to its longevity, low energy usage, and superior brightness, making it an ideal choice for contemporary rail systems.</p></p>
